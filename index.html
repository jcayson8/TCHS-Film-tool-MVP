Help Center

V1.0 workflow guidance.

How does the film workflow work?

Upload clips, verify Team Identity, click Finish Team Review, choose Offense and/or Defense, then label only the queued clips.

Why is a clip missing from Label Queue?

The clip must be marked Offense or Defense, have status Needs Labeling, and be explicitly submitted from Finish Team Review.

How does AI training work?

Each saved coach correction is stored in PostgreSQL and compared with the original AI prediction. The model counts survive Render deployments.

Are Coach Notes generated by guessing?

No. V1.0 Coach Notes use deterministic percentages and counts from labeled plays. When there is not enough data, the tool says so.
